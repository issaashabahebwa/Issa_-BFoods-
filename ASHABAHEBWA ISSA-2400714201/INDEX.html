SAFE RECIPES

Meat is an excellent source of complete protein, meaning it contains all nine essential amino acids necessary for human health. Protein is vital for growth, development, muscle building and repair, and producing enzymes and hormones

Vitamin A: Pumpkin is an excellent source of beta-carotene. Your body converts beta-carotene into Vitamin A, which is crucial for vision, immune function, and skin health. Vitamin C: A good source of this antioxidant vitamin that supports the immune system and skin health.

Mushrooms are unique in the produce aisle as they are the only natural, non-animal food source of Vitamin D. Like humans, they can produce this vitamin when exposed to UV light (either sunlight or a UV lamp). This is converted from a compound called ergosterol.
